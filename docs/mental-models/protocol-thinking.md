**Canonical page**: [Protocol Thinking](https://dev.episkopos.community/docs/mental-models/protocol-thinking)

## Summary

- Identify actors, incentives, and boundaries.
- Map how interactions succeed or break down.
- Apply protocol thinking to software and real-world systems.
